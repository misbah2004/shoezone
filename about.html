Our Story

Crafting timeless textiles with respect for nature, tradition, and the people who make them

Rooted in Tradition

ShoeZone began with a simple belief: that the finest textiles come from understanding the entire journey from seed to stitch. Founded by textile artisans who have spent generations working with natural fibers, we honor the ancient craft of linen making while embracing modern sustainability practices.

Our story is woven into every thread, connecting the timeless traditions of European linen craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibilities. We believe that true luxury lies not in excess, but in the thoughtful consideration of every detail.

Our Journey

From a small family workshop to a respected sustainable fashion brand

The Beginning

2018

Founded in a small European village with a mission to preserve traditional linen craftsmanship while embracing sustainable practices. Started with just three artisans and a commitment to quality.

Sustainable Certification

2020

Achieved GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) certification and partnered with organic flax farms across Europe. Expanded our artisan network to include 20 skilled craftspeople.

Innovation & Growth

2022

Introduced our first home textile collection and launched carbon-neutral shipping worldwide. Reached 50+ artisans and established fair trade partnerships across three countries.

Community Impact

2024

Launched our Artisan Education Program, providing training and resources to the next generation of linen craftspeople. Now working with over 50 artisans and supporting sustainable farming communities.
