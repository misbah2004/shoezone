Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

Machine wash in cold water with mild detergent. Tumble dry on low heat or air dry. Linen naturally softens with each wash and develops a beautiful patina over time. Iron while damp for a crisp look, or embrace the natural wrinkles for a relaxed aesthetic.
